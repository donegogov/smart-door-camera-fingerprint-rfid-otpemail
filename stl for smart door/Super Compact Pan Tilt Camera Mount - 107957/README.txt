Super Compact Pan Tilt Camera Mount by AtomKemp on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:107957

Summary:
This was intended for use on a quad-rotor project, although it can be mounted on really anything. I used two Hitec HS-55 sub-micro servos and it whips the camera around without any problem.
